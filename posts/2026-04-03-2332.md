- The pump case is a new part
  - Before today it did not exist
  - After today, it has:
    - A 101 by 69 mm plate sized for a single Kamoer pump
    - An octagon cutout with four M3 mounting holes around it
    - A 60 mm tower above the plate that steps from octagon to a 37 mm bore
- The cartridge strut is down to one design
  - Before today there were five variants printed for test fitting
  - After today, the bumps are 7.2 mm wide with a 0.6 mm split, and the alternates are gone
- The iOS app recovers after backgrounding
  - Before today, leaving the app for a while left it stuck on the searching screen or with the stats spinner hung
  - After today, returning to the app restarts the scan, clears any half-done image transfer, and retries chart loads up to three times
