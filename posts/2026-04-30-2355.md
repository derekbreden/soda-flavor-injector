- The printed faucet got its forward-bending spout
  - Before today, the dispense tubes ran straight up out of the body and the printed shell stopped where the lever swung
  - After today, it has:
    - A bend that carries the water and two flavor tubes up and out toward you, so soda lands in a glass set in front of the faucet, not under it
    - A printed cap over the lever pocket so the shell is closed where your finger goes
    - Thicker walls around the spout after a first test print showed the join to the cap was weak
    - A wider transition fillet inside the body bore after the first print revealed a fit issue

  - After today, it has:
    - Full VoiceOver and Voice Control across the config screen
    - A small haptic bump when you start a hold-to-prime
