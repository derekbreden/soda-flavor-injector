- Drew the endcaps for the carbonator tank
  - Before today the tank was an open tube with no plan for how to seal it
  - After today, it has:
    - Two laser-cut stainless discs that slip inside the tube for welding
    - Four ports on the top disc for CO2, water in, soda out, and a pressure relief valve
    - A blank bottom disc so the tank sits flat on the counter
- The foam shell got internal bag pockets
  - Before today the shell was a tall tube with no internal structure
  - After today, it has:
    - Three more inches of height to fit the bags and the foam on top
    - Two arc-shaped slots in the floor so the bags drop into the cup below
    - Walls in the bottom cup that pen each bag in its own pocket
    - A connected groove around the rim so the upper and lower halves seat together
