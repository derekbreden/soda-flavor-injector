- Started a new shell for carbonator and bags
  - Before today this part did not exist
  - After today, it has:
    - An inner cylinder hugging the cold core with a 1/4-inch foam gap
    - An outer cylinder with cradle pockets that hold the two flavor bags against the cold side
    - A 1/2-inch foam layer at the bottom and a top platform the bags rest on
    - A split into a bottom cup and an upper shell that nest together so each piece prints without overhangs
- The pump case is closer to a cartridge
  - Before today the top and bottom faces were two different sizes and the wiring had to be fished in by hand
  - After today, it has:
    - A flat 70x70 mm square at top and bottom so all four snap walls have full skirt to grab
    - Snap deflection dialed in by test prints from 2 mm down to 1.5 mm
    - Two stepped bores for the bullet connectors that bring 12 V power through the case wall
    - A written design page for the buckle that locks the cartridge into its dock
- The 3D drawing viewer got an orientation cube
  - Before today you could only orbit the model by dragging with the mouse
  - After today, a labeled cube in the corner snaps the camera to top, front, side, or any other face when you click it
