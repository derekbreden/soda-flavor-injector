# Top Panel — Parts Specification

## Overview

The top panel is a flat panel with no features. It is the last piece inserted
during assembly, sliding down into the top panel channels on the left and right
walls from above (-Z direction).

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Value | Notes |
|-----------|------:|-------|
| Width (X) | 140.0mm | Interior X span between left and right walls |
| Depth (Y) | 123.0mm | Interior Y span between front and back panels |
| Thickness (Z) | 3.0mm | Fits 3.4mm channel; 0.2mm clearance each side |

## Features

1. **Panel body** — flat box, 140.0mm × 123.0mm × 3.0mm. No cutouts, bosses,
   or other features.

## Interfaces

- **Left wall top panel channel:** Z=73.6..77.0mm in wall coordinates (3.4mm
  wide in Z). Panel slides in from above (-Z direction).
- **Right wall top panel channel:** mirror of left wall, same Z range.

## Assembly Notes

- Last piece inserted during assembly — slides down into the top rail channels
  on both walls from above.
- Print orientation: flat on the build plate (Z-axis is the thickness direction).
  No overhangs or support required.

## Print Orientation

Flat on the build plate, Z-axis as the thickness direction. Zero overhangs.
