# Bottom Panel — Parts Specification

## Part Summary

Flat panel. No features. Spans the interior floor of the pump cartridge, captured in the bottom panel channels of the left and right walls.

## Dimensions

| Dimension  | Value   | Derivation |
|------------|---------|------------|
| Width (X)  | 140.0mm | Interior X span between left and right wall interior faces |
| Depth (Y)  | 123.0mm | Interior Y span between front and back panel inner faces (Y=5.0..128.0mm) |
| Thickness (Z) | 3.0mm | Fits 3.4mm channel with 0.2mm clearance each side |

## Wall Rail Interface

The left and right walls each carry a bottom panel channel at Z=2.0..5.4mm (3.4mm wide in Z). The panel slides into these channels from the front (+Y direction) and sits at Z=2.0mm above the wall exterior bottom edge.

- Channel width (Z): 3.4mm
- Panel thickness (Z): 3.0mm
- Clearance each side: 0.2mm

## Features

None. This is a flat panel with no holes, bosses, pockets, or cutouts.

## Print Orientation

Print flat (Z-axis up = panel thickness). The large flat face sits on the build plate. No overhangs, no supports required.

## Assembly Notes

Slides into wall channels from the front opening. No retention features — positional capture comes from the front and back panels once they are installed.
